Features

Everything you need,
right when you need it

Memo Push combines geofencing technology with smart checklists so you're always prepared when it matters most.

Smart Geofences

Draw a boundary around any location — home, office, gym. The moment your phone crosses that boundary, Memo Push springs into action. Supports up to 20 geofences.

Linked Checklists

Create custom checklists for each location. Prioritize critical items, drag to reorder, and link one list to multiple locations. Never leave without what matters.

Instant Push Alerts

Get a push notification the instant you leave, listing everything you haven't checked off. Critical items are highlighted so you never miss what's most important.

Snooze & Reschedule

Not ready yet? Snooze your reminder for 5, 15, 30 minutes or an hour. Your session is preserved — checked items stay checked when the reminder fires again.

Completion History

Track how often you've left with everything on your list. Spot patterns, improve habits, and stay accountable with your full session history.

Private by Design

All reminders fire on-device — no server needed. Your checklists and location data stay on your phone. No cloud processing required for notifications.

How It Works

Set it once.
Never forget again.

Getting started takes less than two minutes. After that, Memo Push runs silently in the background until you need it.

1

Create a geofence

Search for your address or drop a pin on the map. Drag to set the boundary radius (50 m–2 km). Name it "Home", "Office", or anything you like.

2

Build your checklist

Add items you always need when leaving this location — keys, charger, medication, lunch. Mark critical items so they stand out in the notification.

3

Link checklist to location

Connect your checklist to the geofence. One checklist can cover multiple locations, or each location can have its own unique list.

4

Walk out — get reminded

The moment you cross the geofence boundary, a notification lists everything unchecked. Tap to open the list, check items, and go — all in seconds.
