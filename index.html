Features

Everything you need,
right when you need it

Smart geofencing meets effortless checklists so you're always prepared the moment it counts.

Smart Geofences

Draw a boundary around any location — home, office, gym. The instant your phone crosses it, Memo Push springs into action. Up to 20 locations.

Linked Checklists

Create custom lists for each location. Mark critical items, drag to reorder, and link one list to multiple places. Never leave without what matters.

Instant Push Alerts

Get a notification the instant you leave, listing every unchecked item. Critical items are highlighted so you never miss what's most important.

Snooze & Reschedule

Not ready? Snooze 5, 15, 30 min or an hour. Your session is preserved — checked items stay checked when the reminder fires again.

Completion History

Track how often you've left fully prepared. Spot patterns, improve habits, stay accountable with your full session history.

Private by Design

All reminders fire on-device. Your location data stays on your phone — no cloud processing required for notifications.

How It Works

Set it once.
Never forget again.

Getting started takes under two minutes. After that, Memo Push runs silently until you need it.

1

Create a geofence

Search your address, drop a pin, and drag the boundary to set the radius (50 m–2 km). Name it anything.

2

Build your checklist

Add items you always need when leaving. Mark critical ones so they stand out in your notification.

3

Link list to location

Connect your checklist to the geofence. One list can cover multiple locations.

4

Walk out — get reminded

The moment you cross the boundary, a notification lists everything unchecked. Tap, check, go.
